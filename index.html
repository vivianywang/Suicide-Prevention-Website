About

Suicide, put simply, means ending your own life. It is often a way for people to escape pain or suffering. Suicide is a huge cause of death all around the world with almost 2000 people being a victim of is in one single day. And it could all be prevented if we just paid a little bit more attention.

Common Misconceptions

Suicide is most common in teens - Although suicide is one of the highest death causes in teens, suicide is more common with middle aged people.

Suicide comes without warning - There are many signs that someone could pick out that could be related to one's mental health. Some examples include less eating, less socializing with friends, more isolation, and habit changes.
